About

Everything's quiet in black and white

When I started photography this winter the colors outside where dull, the overcast skies made everything darker, more grey. I waited for summer with excitment so I could finnaly bring collor to my work. But sudenly trying out black and white presets, I become somewhat obsessed. They tolled me more than any other picture, it translated emotion, it felt quiet, as if the other pictures where shouting at me.
